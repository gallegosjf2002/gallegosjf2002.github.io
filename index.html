Selected Research & Projects

Relativistic Polytrope Model

Analytical and numerical study of energy exchange between a relativistic polytrope and a Wyman IIa fluid, based on solutions of the Einstein Field Equations.

Includes stability analysis, causality constraints, and violation regimes of the Strong Energy Condition (K < 0.43).

Magnetic Field Simulation

Finite-element modeling and GPU-accelerated simulation of Helmholtz and crossed-coil magnetic systems for high-precision irradiators.

Includes numerical stability analysis, 3D field visualization, and experimental validation.

Avatar Tech

Satellite-data visualization platform integrating GPM, MODIS, and SMAP datasets for climate resilience and education.

Developed for NASA Space Apps Challenge. Focused on accessibility and environmental decision-making.

Hawa Pachata – Exoplanetarium

Interactive 3D exoplanetarium developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024. Designed to bring astronomy education to underserved and indigenous communities through immersive virtual environments, VR, and real NASA data.
