Research Statement

My research focuses on relativistic systems governed by Einstein’s field equations. I am particularly interested in anisotropic fluids, gravitational decoupling methods, and stability conditions in compact objects.

I aim to bridge mathematical physics with astrophysical observables through computational modeling and numerical simulations.
